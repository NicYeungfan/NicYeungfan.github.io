About

Dr. Fan Yang is a Postdoctoral Fellow at PolyU. He develops AI-assisted ultrasound methods and intelligent sensing systems for battery diagnostics and remote health. His publications include Applied Energy, Journal of Energy Storage, and Remote Sensing.

Research Focus

Battery Diagnostics
Ultrasound-guided SoC/SoH, TOF/dispersion features, EKF/Bayesian fusion.
Remote Vascular Screening
Edge AI, interpretable signal pipelines, low-cost probes for communities.
Physics-Informed Learning
Biot/poroelastic priors, multi-path beamforming, uncertainty calibration.

About Me

I received my Master's and Ph.D. degrees from the Hong Kong Polytechnic University. My main research interests include battery health monitoring, ultrasonic transducer applications, and using intelligent algorithms to solve practical problems.

Research Interests

  • Intelligent algorithms in ultrasonic and battery health monitoring applications
  • Ultrasound applications in industry and medical imaging
  • Ultrasonic applications in battery health diagnostics
